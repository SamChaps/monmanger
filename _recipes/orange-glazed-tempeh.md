<div class="lang-en-content" markdown="1">

Pan-fried tempeh cubes glazed in a quick sauce of orange marmalade, vegetable broth, soy sauce, and sriracha. Simple, quick, and delicious enough to win over even the most skeptical tempeh doubters. Serve over basmati rice.

## Ingredients

- <span class="qty">3</span> tbsp vegetable oil
- <span class="qty">240</span> g (<span class="qty">8.5</span> oz) tempeh, cut into small cubes
- <span class="qty">1</span> tbsp ginger, minced
- <span class="qty">2</span> cloves garlic, minced
- <span class="qty">3</span> green onions, chopped
- <span class="qty">250</span> ml (<span class="qty">1</span> cup) vegetable broth
- <span class="qty">60</span> ml (<span class="qty">1/4</span> cup) orange marmalade
- <span class="qty">2</span> tbsp soy sauce
- <span class="qty">1/2</span> tsp sriracha sauce
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions

1. **Pan-fry** the tempeh cubes in oil in a skillet over high heat for 5 minutes until golden.
2. **Add** the ginger, garlic, and green onions. Cook 2 minutes more.
3. **Stir** in the vegetable broth, marmalade, soy sauce, and sriracha. Continue cooking until the broth is absorbed and the sauce is glossy.
4. **Season** with salt to taste and serve over basmati rice.

## Notes & Tips

- Orange marmalade is the key; it creates a beautiful sticky glaze as it reduces.
- Reduce the sriracha or omit it entirely for a milder version.
- Tempeh is one of the most nutritious plant-based protein sources.

Des cubes de tempeh poêles et enrobés d'une sauce rapide à la marmelade d'orange, bouillon de légumes, sauce soya et sriracha. Simple, rapide et assez délicieux pour convertir les plus sceptiques au tempeh. Servir sur du riz basmati.

- <span class="qty">60</span> ml (<span class="qty">1/4</span> tasse) de marmelade d'orange
- <span class="qty">2</span> c. à soupe de sauce soya

3. **Incorporer** le bouillon, la marmelade, la sauce soya et la sriracha. Poursuivre la cuisson jusqu'à ce que le bouillon soit absorbe et la sauce brillante.
4. **Saler** au goût et servir sur du riz basmati.
